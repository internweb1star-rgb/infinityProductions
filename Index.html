View some of our frequently asked questions — they might take your concerns away!

How can I get started with VR Development?+
Getting started is simple — reach out via our contact form and we'll schedule a free consulting call. We walk you through the process, assess your needs and propose the best technical path forward, whether that's VR, AR or a hybrid approach.
What is VR App Development?+
VR App Development is the process of creating software applications that run inside virtual reality headsets like Meta Quest, HTC Vive or Apple Vision Pro. These apps create fully immersive 3D environments for training, simulation, entertainment or marketing.
How does AR App Development work?+
AR development overlays digital content onto the real world, typically via a smartphone camera or AR glasses. We build AR apps using platforms like ARKit, ARCore and Unity, integrating 3D models, animations and interactive overlays into your environment.
How long does a typical project take?+
Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A focused AR proof-of-concept might take 6–8 weeks, while a full-scale VR training platform could take 4–6 months. We provide clear timelines during our discovery and planning phase.
Do you work with startups or only enterprise clients?+
We work with both. Our client base ranges from funded startups building their first MVP to Fortune 500 companies like Microsoft and Ford deploying complex immersive experiences. We tailor our approach to match your budget and goals.
